Kushani's Eggplant

Home

Description

A friend gave us this recipe for a vegan, indian inspired, eggplant sheet bake. It serves as a great meal prep for our week, usually yielding enough to feed 4-6 people.Thats a lot of time freed up for the void.

Ingredients

  • Garbanzo beans
  • Tomatoes
  • Gram Massala
  • The phattest aubergine money can buy

Steps

  1. Shove garlic bulbs into eggplant
  2. Oil and season eggplant
  3. Bake for like forever. Until it is chared and collapsing. Usually 30-45 minutes, flipping throughout.
