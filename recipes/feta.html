Description

This is another easy one. The goal is to have enough saucy veggies and protein to get through the week. It is my liferaft in this post-capitalist hellscape.

Technically this is Mediterranean, so it probably makes you live longer. Eat at your own risk.

Ingredients

  • Block of feta
  • Garbanzo beans
  • Tomatoes
  • All the veggies

Steps

  1. Throw it all on a pan
  2. Throw it in the oven
  3. Take it out of the oven
